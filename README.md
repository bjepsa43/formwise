## What Does FormWise Actually Do?

The short version: it turns your prompts into apps.

You design a SmartForm — basically a structured input form wired to your AI prompt logic — and FormWise generates a clean, branded tool your end users can interact with. They fill in a few fields, hit submit, and get an AI-generated output that follows your exact framework. No blank ChatGPT box. No prompt engineering on their end. Just your expertise, delivered on demand.

The other main product type is the **CoPilot** — a guided chatbot that follows your frameworks and brand voice, available 24/7. Think of it as a trained assistant that sounds like you, scaled to infinity.

Both tool types can be:

- Embedded on any website, landing page, or CRM via iFrame
- Bundled into **Toolsets** (a portfolio-style page hosting multiple tools)
- Gated behind Stripe-powered logins so you can charge for access
- White-labeled with your own logo, colors, and custom CSS

One thing a lot of users don't realize until they're deep in the platform: FormWise also bundles in a **free GoHighLevel (GHL) subaccount** with every paid plan. That's a full CRM, funnel builder, email/SMS campaigns, and membership hosting — normally $97/month on its own — just included. For agencies already on GHL, this is a genuinely big deal.

## The Features That Actually Matter

**Prompt Chaining** — You can link multiple prompts together into a workflow. Output from step one feeds into step two, and so on. This is how you build tools that do more than one thing — like an onboarding flow that generates a custom strategy, then a follow-up email, then a task list.

**Data Sources** — Upload PDFs, paste in URLs, drop in TXT files. Your tools can reference your own content, client documents, or proprietary frameworks. The AI gives context-aware responses instead of generic ones.

**ThemeBuilder** — A relatively new feature that lets you style your tools to match your brand completely. Colors, fonts, layouts — no CSS required if you don't want it, but full CSS access if you do.

**Login Mode** — Gate your tools behind Stripe-powered plans. Free tier, paid tier, usage-based credits — you control the monetization model. This is what turns your tools from "cool demos" into actual recurring revenue products.

**50+ Language Real-Time Translation** — Your tools can serve users in their native language, which matters a lot if you're running global campaigns or working with international clients.

**AI Tool Builder Assistant** — Tell the AI assistant what kind of tool you want to build, and it generates a starting point for you. Pro users get up to 20 tools/month this way; Agency users get 125/month.
